Setup IPTV on Fire TV Stick

Follow the simple steps below to setup STB EMU app to connect with NexaTV on your Fire TV stick or Fire TV.

1. Change developer options:

Fire stick Settings –> My Firetv –> Click Developer options –> Click Apps from Unknown Sources to turn it on –> Click Turn On

2. Go to amazon app store, find downloader app and install it

3. Start downloader and enter this url and click download – https://bit.ly/3wPpeVb

Once the download is complete, install STB EMU app. You will need the MAC ID which starts with 00:1A:79 located under STB settings of the app to start streaming NexaTV.
